Sole Technical Owner

The 4-Phase Approach
Every connection goes through four phases. No shortcuts.
Phase 01
Week 1-2
Warm Up
  • Find their 3 most recent posts
  • Comment on 2-3 posts — quality, anchored to details
  • Like or react to 2-3 more
  • Result: Seen 5+ times before any message
Cold = 15% • After warmup = 50%+
Phase 02
Week 2-3
Connect
  • Personalized connection request
  • Reference specific content they posted
  • 3-5 requests per day, never more
  • Space them 4-7 minutes apart
Phase 03
Week 3-4
Conversation
  • Value-first DM within 48 hours
  • Hiring managers: question about team/stack
  • Recruiters: intro + credentials
  • Peers: real technical conversation
No pitching in first DM
Phase 04
Week 4+
Opportunity
  • Mention open to roles — warm, not cold
  • Weekly: 2-3 comments on connections
  • Monthly: value DM
  • Let them come to him. Inbound.

Section 07
Golden Rules
Follow these to protect the account and maximize results

Daily Limit: 10-15 Comments

Arkesh has 635 followers. New account limits apply. Quality over quantity. Never push past 15 in a single day.

Never Copy-Paste

Always paraphrase and type manually. LinkedIn's detection systems flag identical text patterns across comments.

Time Gaps Matter

4-7 minutes between comments. Longer breaks after every 3. Two sessions per day: morning and evening.

Never Pitch in Comments

Comments build visibility and trust. Pitching happens in DMs only — and only after Phase 3. No exceptions.

Anchor to Specifics

Every comment references a specific detail from the post — a number, a tool, a quote. Generic comments are invisible.

If Analytics Drop

Profile views or engagement drops more than 30% week-over-week? Pause all activity for 48 hours. Don't push through.
